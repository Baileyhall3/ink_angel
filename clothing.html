IRRELEVANT CLOTHING


Ink Angel Tattoo studio was founded in 2012 by the original Ink Angel - Gayle. It had always been a dream of hers to open her own tattoo studio one day, after falling in love with art at a young age and then working in several tattoo studios before starting the Ink Angel project.


Together with her brother Kyle, who took ownership of the studio in 2023, Ink angel quickly established themselves as one of the most reputable tattoo studios in South Shields.
